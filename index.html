Who We Are

We are The Horror Hounds — a pack of horror-loving misfits based in Garden Grove, CA (Orange County).

We provide a safe place for folks to be their authentic selves. We love horror, sci-fi, and all things nerdy. From movie nights at The Frida Cinema to goth club outings, beach bonfires to book clubs — if it's spooky, fun, and a little weird, we're there.

Whether you're into slashers, found footage, psychological thrillers, or horror comedies — you'll find your people here. Come hang with us.
